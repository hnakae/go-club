[aga-learn to play](https://www.usgo.org/content.aspx?page_id=22&club_id=454497&module_id=550339)

Recommended order:

1. Learn the Rules of Go
2. Learn ladders and nets with capture go

3. Learn some shapes and components: bamboo, net, tiger, dog, horse made by one space jumps, diagonals, and knight's moves.
4. Play real 9x9 game and Count the Score.

5. Learn first two star point josekis
6. Look at some professional games (shusaku/dosaku/goseigen/kitaniminoru/ChoChikun1980-87/TakemiyaMasaki/NieWeiping/ChoHunhyun1988/KobayashiKoichi1989/LeeChangho1991-2006/MaXiaochun/GuLi/LeeSedol2006-2010/ParkJungwhan2012-2014/KeJie2014-2016/alphagoLee2016/alphagoMaster/alphagoZero/ShinJinseo2018-now/andOthers:GuZihao,LianXiao,FanTingyu, YangDingxin,ShinMinjun,XieErhao,FanYunruo)
7. Puzzles: https://tsumego-hero.com/sets/view/50

8. use: 
  - 2 star points opening 
  - 2 josekis (knight's move response & 1 space pincer)
  - keima approach
  -  https://online-go.com/play
